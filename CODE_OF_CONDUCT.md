# Code of Conduct

## Scope

This policy applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, security reports, shared research artifacts, and other project spaces. Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior threatens people, credentials, research integrity, or trading safety.

## Standards

Contributors must communicate respectfully, discuss evidence rather than personalities, disclose material assumptions and conflicts, and avoid claims of guaranteed investment returns. Harassment, credential solicitation, market manipulation, deceptive performance presentation, cherry-picked evidence presented as independent validation, and promotion of unreviewed live-trading changes are not accepted.

Report conduct or security concerns privately through GitHub Security Advisories when public disclosure could cause harm.
